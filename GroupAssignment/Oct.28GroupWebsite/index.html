Nicole, Manny, Peter and Quinlan are a great group of friends who very much enjoy the fall season. They enjoy hanging out with each other.

There are many reasons why fall is such an amazing season. The breeze that comes with the slowly creeping winter, the colours of orange, yellow and red everywhere you go. It’s the time to wear warm, comfortable clothes. Don’t forget piling leaves to play in. There are also fairs and festivals to go to where all the unhealthy but amazing food is, like taffy, pumpkin pie, fudge, cotton candy and especially candy corn! Thanksgiving is also a great event that happens in the fall time, for college students it is definitely the best event of the year.

Then there is the spooky time of Halloween, where kids get to dress up as their favourite characters and go door to door collecting their favourite types of candy, saying those famous words “Trick or treat!” That doesn’t mean that adults don’t get to indulge in the fun as well; there are Halloween parties to attend where adults can act as kids again and also dress as their favourite characters without it being weird.

In conclusion, fall is a great season and definitely in the top two favourite seasons for Nicole, Peter, Manny and Quinlan.
